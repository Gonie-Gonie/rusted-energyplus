# v0.4.0 Plan

Purpose: time, weather, and schedule evidence for the declared early subset.

Evidence targets:

- `RunPeriod` typed intake
- EnergyPlus-style hourly time-axis expansion
- EPW parsing for dry-bulb, dew point, relative humidity, pressure, radiation,
  and wind fields
- ESO comparison for selected EPW weather fields
- `Schedule:Constant` ESO comparison
- `Schedule:Compact` all-days `Until` segment intake and hourly evaluation
- case manifests and baseline-only report skeletons for schedule and weather

This milestone is about aligned time-series smoke evidence. It does not claim
heat-balance, HVAC, plant, full schedule-calendar conformance, or a
tolerance-gated conformance release until reports and blocking gates are
promoted accordingly.

The v0.4 weather list is split deliberately:

- comparison smoke: dry-bulb, dew point, relative humidity, barometric
  pressure, wind speed, and wind direction
- parser-only weather fields: horizontal infrared radiation, global horizontal
  radiation, direct normal radiation, and diffuse horizontal radiation
- future solar/radiation ESO diagnostics: Site Direct Solar Radiation Rate per
  Area, Site Diffuse Solar Radiation Rate per Area, Site Solar Altitude Angle,
  and Site Solar Azimuth Angle

No v0.4 weather or schedule case is promoted to conformance. Promotion requires
a case manifest with `conformance_claim = true`, declared tolerances,
Rust result artifacts, `compare-summary.json`, `compare-report.md`, and a
blocking gate.
