Evidence targets:

- EnergyPlus-style hourly time-axis expansion
- EPW parsing for dry-bulb, dew point, relative humidity, pressure, radiation,

heat-balance, HVAC, plant, full schedule-calendar conformance, or a
tolerance-gated conformance release until reports and blocking gates are
promoted accordingly.
