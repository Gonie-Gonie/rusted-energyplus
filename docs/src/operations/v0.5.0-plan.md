# v0.5.0 Plan

Purpose: static geometry, construction, and internal-gain evidence for input
interpretation, without claiming heat-balance or HVAC numerical conformance.

Evidence targets:

- EIO `Zone Information` comparison for zone surface count, floor area,
  volume, and exterior gross wall area
- EIO `HeatTransfer Surface` comparison for surface class, net/gross area,
  azimuth, and tilt
- EIO `Construction CTF` and `Material CTF Summary` comparison for the first
  construction/material thermal-input subset
- EIO `OtherEquipment Internal Gains Nominal` comparison for nominal internal
  gains and convective fraction
- ESO `Zone Total Internal Convective Heating Rate` trace comparison for the
  first internal-gain runtime series
- `surface_geometry_001` case manifest using static output requests for surface
  geometry evidence

This milestone is about input/static evidence and nominal internal-gain
plumbing. It does not claim surface heat-transfer, zone air temperature,
fenestration, shading, coordinate-system variants, HVAC, plant conformance, or
a tolerance-gated conformance release until reports and blocking gates are
promoted accordingly.
