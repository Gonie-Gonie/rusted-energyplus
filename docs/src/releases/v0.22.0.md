# v0.22.0 Release Notes

v0.22.0 closes the Time, Weather, and Schedule Conformance Expansion
milestone.

This release promotes two explicitly declared hourly variables:

- `Schedule Value` in `schedule_constant_001`
- `Site Outdoor Air Drybulb Temperature` in `weather_fields_001`

The new gate is timestamp-aligned against EnergyPlus ESO hourly labels and
writes `compare-report.md` plus `compare-summary.json` artifacts under
`.runtime/time-weather-schedule-conformance/26.1.0`.

## Added

- `conformance time-weather-schedule-report`
- `compare-schedule-conformance`
- `compare-weather-conformance`
- v0.22 coverage in the numeric conformance evidence PDF/HTML/JSON

## Claim Boundary

The v0.22 claim is declared-variables-only. It does not claim general runtime
compatibility, HVAC compatibility, plant compatibility, meter conformance, or
weather conformance beyond the declared dry-bulb series.

In `weather_fields_001`, dew point, relative humidity, barometric pressure,
wind speed, and wind direction remain diagnostic rows.
