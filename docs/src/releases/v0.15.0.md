## Scope

Included:

- `plant_loop_diagnostic_001` diagnostic-only conformance case based on a
  plant-only PlantLoadProfile fixture.
- `plant-state` and `plant-equipment` output classes.
- baseline-only EnergyPlus oracle rows for plant supply-side demand/state,
  pump electricity, district heating rate, and load profile heat transfer.
- `plant-loop-diagnostic-smoke` verification of manifest, zero-warning oracle
  baseline, staged epJSON compile/plan, and baseline-only report summary.
- release PDF/HTML/JSON evidence pack for the promoted v0.8/v0.9 numerical
  conformance cases.

Conformance claims included in this release are still limited to earlier
declared tolerance-gated cases:

- `heat_balance_nomass_001`: `Zone Mean Air Temperature`
- `surface_temperature_nomass_001`: zone MAT plus surface inside/outside face
  temperatures

## Not Included

v0.15.0 does not claim:

- plant numerical conformance
- plant loop flow balancing or operation-scheme parity
- pump electricity, district heating, boiler load, chiller load, or
  condenser-loop parity
- plant node temperature, mass-flow, setpoint, or plant meter parity
- plant ExampleFiles compatibility
- HVAC, node, IdealLoads, sizing, autosizing, availability-manager, or general
  ExampleFiles compatibility beyond already promoted cases

The plant diagnostic case remains:

```text
comparison_class: diagnostic-only
conformance_claim: false
tolerance_policy: none
status: baseline-only
```
