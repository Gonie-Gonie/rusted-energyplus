## Scope

Included:

- `versioning-reset-v2.md` canonical roadmap reset.
- `legacy-milestones.md` classification of v0.1 through v0.15 as Historical
  Pre-Alpha Evidence Series.
- rewritten `milestone-map.md` around Road to v1.0 and v1/v2/v3 targets.
- `v1-scope.md`, `v2-full-compatibility.md`, and `v3-fast-successor.md`.
- v0.17 and v0.18 plan seeds for manifest v2, output request schema, output
  injection, and oracle baseline pipeline.
- `run plant-state-projection` diagnostic command as an additional addendum,
  not as the official v0.16 milestone purpose.
- release PDF/HTML/JSON evidence pack for the promoted v0.8/v0.9 numerical
  conformance cases.

Conformance claims included in this release are still limited to earlier
declared tolerance-gated cases:

- `heat_balance_nomass_001`: `Zone Mean Air Temperature`
- `surface_temperature_nomass_001`: zone MAT plus surface inside/outside face
  temperatures

## Claim Boundary

v0.16.0 does not claim:

- a new numerical conformance case.
- full EnergyPlus compatibility.
- broad ExampleFiles compatibility.
- IdealLoads, node, HVAC, plant, meter, sizing, or autosizing conformance.
- plant loop flow balancing or operation-scheme parity.
- pump electricity, district heating, load profile, boiler load, chiller load,
  condenser-loop, or plant node-state parity.

The Rust plant projection addendum remains:

```text
comparison_class: diagnostic-only
conformance_claim: false
algorithm_parity: false
tolerance_policy: none
status: projected
```
